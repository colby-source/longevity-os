# Longevity OS — Set It Up in Your Own Claude (10 min)

Your data stays in **your** Claude account. Nobody else sees it. You're using Claude as the engine that reads your files and builds your dashboard.

---

## Step 1 — Pick your path

### Path A — Claude Pro (best, reusable)
1. Go to **claude.ai** → **Projects** → **New Project** → name it "Longevity OS".
2. Open **Custom Instructions** → paste all of **`PROJECT_INSTRUCTIONS.md`**.
3. Add Project files: **`dashboard-template.html`** (the design Claude will match) and optionally **`knowledge-starter.md`**.
4. Done — your coach is ready and remembers everything.

### Path B — Free (no Project)
1. Go to **claude.ai** → **New chat**.
2. Paste the prompt from **`MASTER_PROMPT.md`**.
3. Attach your files (Step 3) and send.

---

## Step 2 — Gather your data (one-time exports)

**WHOOP** — WHOOP app → **More** → **App Settings** → **Data Export** → **Request Export** → they email you a **ZIP of CSVs**.

**Genetics**
- 23andMe: 23andme.com → **Settings** → **"23andMe Data"** → **Download** → **Raw data** (`.txt`).
- AncestryDNA: Settings → **Download DNA Data**.
- Sequencing.com: log in → **Files** → download your report exports.

**Viome** — Viome app → **Results** → **share/download** → **Save PDF**.

**Labs** — LabCorp (patient.labcorp.com → Results → **Download PDF**), Quest (MyQuest → Results → **Download**), Rythm/other (open results → **Print → Save as PDF**). No portal? A clear **photo** of each page works.

**Supplements/peptides** — just **snap a photo** of each label.

---

## Step 3 — Upload + build

Attach your files to the chat (you can add them in batches) and say:

> **"Build my dashboard."**

Claude parses everything, builds your personalized dashboard as an **Artifact** (open it, download it, share it), and writes your analysis: biological age, top flags, genetics→monitoring, overlaps, and your top moves.

---

## Tips
- **File size:** raw whole-genome files are huge — upload your **genetic report exports** or the **23andMe/Ancestry raw `.txt`**, not a full genome. WHOOP CSVs and lab PDFs are fine.
- **Partial is fine:** upload only what you have. Claude labels the rest "no data yet."
- **Keep it current:** upload a new lab panel anytime and say **"update my dashboard and tell me what changed."**
- **It's your coach:** ask it anything — "why is my HRV low?", "what should I fix first?", "any overlaps in my stack?"
